FAQ

The questions we hear most often from first-time customers.

How long is the voucher valid?

12 months from the purchase date. You can book the flight at any time during that period using our calendar at akrobacja.com/kalendarz.

Where is the airport? Can I get there from abroad?

Radom-Piastów airport (EPRP), 100 km from Warsaw, about 90 minutes by car. We can recommend hotels in Radom for guests travelling internationally — Warsaw Chopin (WAW) and Warsaw Modlin (WMI) are the closest airports.

What is the weight limit for passengers?

110 kg. Above that the Extra 300L's CG envelope is exceeded. Minimum age is 13 (parental consent required under 18). No prior flying experience needed.

What if the weather is bad?

We reschedule for free, no questions asked. If we can't find a new date within the voucher's 12-month validity, we refund 100%.

Who is the pilot?

Maciej Kulaszewski — 2022 World Aerobatic Champion, multiple-time Polish Champion, instructor FI(A), 3 500+ hours on the Extra 300L. He flies every voucher flight personally.

Can I add a video recording of my flight?

Yes — the Video 360° add-on (+299 PLN) gives you a 90-second edited MP4 from the onboard and external cameras, delivered within 48 hours. Tick the box on the checkout form.
