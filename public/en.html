Safety

Why flying with a champion
is safer than you think

🎯

Aircraft mastery

3,000+ hours on the Extra 300L — one of the most resilient aerobatic aircraft in the world. Maciej knows every behaviour of this machine in every flight configuration. An airline pilot flies safely in normal conditions. Maciej knows how to maintain control in any conditions.

📐

Safety margins

Passenger flights are flown with margins many times greater than in competition. Figures are performed at safe altitudes, at speeds that ensure full control. Your flight uses just 30% of the aircraft's and pilot's capabilities.

🛡️

Emergency training

Certified FI(A) flight instructor and aerobatic judge. Thousands of hours of training covering emergency procedures, spins and recovery. A World Champion leaves nothing to chance.
